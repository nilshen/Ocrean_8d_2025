How to Play

Welcome to the World Under the Sea!

Spongebob's birthday is in 2 days, move your MOUSE around the screen to recycle any GARBAGES that are throwed into the sea!

You will score 1 point for each item recycled but stay away from the SHARKS because you only have 3 LIVES.

At the meantime, try not to let GARBAGES hit the SHARKS because they are also part of the ecosystem!

You may click the mouse to exchange 10 scores to 1 LIFE!
